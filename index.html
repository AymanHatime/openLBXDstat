open LDXD stat

Welcome to my website ! It permits you to get more statistics about your Letterboxd account. This is a personnal & unnoficial project. It have no affiliation with Letterboxd. All you have to do is to export your data from Letterboxd, and then upload the zip file here. The website will process the data and give you access to a dashboard with various statistics about your account. More than you can get from the free plan of Letterboxd. Of course, none of your data will be stored on the server, everything is processed locally in your browser. This project is open source, you can find the code on GitHub.
